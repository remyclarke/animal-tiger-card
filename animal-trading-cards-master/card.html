Tiger

Here's some interesting facts about Tigers

  1. Tigers are excellent swimmers and enjoy bathing in rivers and ponds. They are one of the few cat species that enjoy being in the water.
  2. Tigers are solitary animals and are highly territorial. They have been known to mark their territory with urine and by scratching trees.
  3. Tigers are the largest of all big cats and can weigh up to 600 pounds (272 kg).
  4. Tigers have very distinctive stripes, which are like fingerprints and are unique to each individual.
  5. Tigers are carnivores, eating only meat. They mainly feed on large mammals such as deer, wild pigs, antelope and buffalo.

Tigers are large, carnivorous felines that belong to the Panthera genus. They are known for their strikingly beautiful orange and black striped fur and powerful build. Tigers are found across Asia, in countries such as India, Russia, and Indonesia, but their populations have declined significantly due to habitat loss and hunting.
